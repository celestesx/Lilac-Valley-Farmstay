About Us

Welcome to Lilac Valley Farm Stay, a genuine 80 year old barn lovingly restored by Marina Ye, an interior designer residing in Sydney, New South Wales. Standing elegantly on our magnificent 4 acre property in the hills of the beautiful Blue Mountains in New South Wales, Lilac Valley Farm Stay is a truly unique and luxurious private home. Perfect for couples, groups and families alike, Lilac Valley Farm Stay houses 8 guests comfortably and ideal for a romantic getaway, family holiday or celebrations.

Located just 10 minutes from the town centre of Katoomba, Lilac Valley Farm Stay is surrounded by the magnificent Blue Mountains, perfect for a base after your day's adventure. On the property, the farm has sheep, chickens, ducks and our resident friendly goat, Rosie. Our guests can experience farm life and rural living on Lilac Valley's expansive compound.

The Barn's History

Lilac Valley Farm Stay has a rich history of farming and has been privately owned for over 80 years. It was originally a working sheep and cattle farm. Using original Jarrah timber accents and beams, sustainability was a priority while recycling original parts of the barn during the renovation process.

The development and interior attended to by Marina Ye focuses on craftsmanship and artisanal production with custom-made furniture, cabinetry, and upholstery.Minimalism as the main focus, the entire barn is furnished with the finest finishes and comfortable luxuries to reflect the entire mood of the barn.
